Vulnerability Demonstration: PoisonTap (Lab)

Built a controlled lab demonstration of PoisonTap on a Raspberry Pi Zero to illustrate how an emulated USB Ethernet device can intercept HTTP traffic and capture session cookies. The write‑up documents setup at a high level and, importantly, defensive measures (enforce HTTPS, clear cookies, disable USB autorun, lock screen policies). Conducted on my own equipment for education and defensive security awareness only.
